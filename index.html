Hello, we're The Rabbit Hole, your new saving grace for our bunnies. We strive to provide for as many of our furry friends possible. With our care and support, we can give them the best lives, that's our promise!

Up to 365 days/year

Our staff knows how much these lives mean to you! With our subscribtion plan, you can provide food, shelter, and medical care up to 365days/year coverage. You can choose to order a more flexible plan if that's your style.

Raised not caged

Your only a few clicks away from giving our furry friends the care they deserve. All of our bunnies are fed 100% organic locally sourced pellets and treats.

100% Organic

All of our vegetables are fresh, organic and local. Bunnies are cared for without feed that has added hormones or antibiotics. Good for their health, the environment, and they extra love the carrot treats!

Support your way

We don't limit your creativity, which means you can support however you'd like! You can also choose from our special support program to recieve picture updates on bunnies who need the most care!
